Family Photos



1. Sarah Euphemia Wylie, known as "Phemie", "Pheme" or "Gammy". Wife of Alex Huston. She was born on August 31st, 1878 at Cresswell, Ontario. She was married on October 7th, 1905 in Toronto and died on November 27th, 1959 in Lindsay, Ontario. In this photograph she is 60 - 61 years of age. The photograph was probably taken in 1938.


2. Alex Huston's children Margaret, William and Robert Huston.


3. Robbie, Maggie Aggie and Buddy.


4. Robert Wylie Huston (photo of him younger with photo of him older to the right)


5. William Thomas Huston in school photo in Toronto. He is middle row center in coat with black turtleneck underneath.


6. William Thomas Huston "Bud"


7. Margaret Agnes Huston "Gamma" age 24 in the Toronto Star on September 22, 1932 re. canning goods for the hungry in the great depression. I think of her often and miss her much! One of her best life-long friends, Jean Waldon, is to her left.
